Eligibility Criteria

To ensure the benefits reach those who need it most, the scheme has specific eligibility requirements:

Permanent Resident of West Bengal
Applicant must be above 18 years of age
Annual household income below prescribed limit
Not a beneficiary of other similar schemes

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common queries about the Annapurna Bhandar scheme.

How do I track my application status?
You can track your application status by logging into the official portal using your registered mobile number and Aadhar ID.
When will the monthly benefit be credited?
The ₹3000 benefit is typically credited to the verified bank account between the 1st and 7th of every month.
Is there any application fee?
No, the application for Annapurna Bhandar is completely free of charge at all government offices and online portals.
Can I apply if I have a job?
Yes, as long as your total household income falls within the eligibility criteria defined by the scheme guidelines.
