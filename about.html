Our Mission

Dedicated to Citizen Welfare

Annapurna Bhandar Information Portal was created with a single goal: to bridge the gap between government welfare schemes and the citizens who need them most.

We believe that information is the first step towards empowerment. Many eligible citizens miss out on vital financial assistance simply because they lack clear guidance on how to apply or what documents are required.

What We Provide

  • Accurate and up-to-date information about the Annapurna Bhandar scheme.
  • Step-by-step guides for registration and document verification.
  • Quick links to official government portals and forms.
  • A comprehensive FAQ section to resolve common citizen queries.
